Beach and Coastal Safari

Kenya’s coastline offers some of the most beautiful beaches in the world.

Diani Beach

Relax on the pristine white sands and enjoy water sports like kite surfing, scuba diving, and snorkeling.

Mombasa Beach

Explore the vibrant coastal city of Mombasa, enjoy fresh seafood, and take a boat trip to nearby islands like Wasini.

Lamu Island

Step back in time and discover the ancient Swahili architecture and tranquil beaches on this UNESCO World Heritage island.

Historical Safari

Kenya’s history is rich and varied. From ancient ruins to colonial landmarks, explore the cultural and historical heritage of this diverse country.

Fort Jesus

Visit the 16th-century Portuguese fort in Mombasa, which offers a glimpse into Kenya's colonial past.

Gedi Ruins

Explore the ancient ruins of Gedi, a Swahili town that dates back to the 12th century, offering insights into Kenya's past.

Karen Blixen Museum

p>Visit the former home of the famed author of *Out of Africa* and explore the colonial history and lifestyle of early settlers.

Adventure Safari

For thrill-seekers, Kenya offers a range of exciting activities, from climbing mountains to rafting down rivers and more.

Mount Kenya Climbing

Climb Africa's second-highest peak and enjoy breathtaking views from the top of Mount Kenya, a challenging and rewarding adventure.

White-water Rafting

Experience the thrill of white-water rafting on the Tana River, navigating through scenic rapids in Kenya's beautiful wilderness.

Hot Air Ballooning

Take to the skies with a hot air balloon ride over the Maasai Mara, offering panoramic views of the savannah and wildlife.

Cultural Safari

Kenya’s diverse cultures and traditions offer a unique and immersive experience for those interested in learning more about its people, music, and arts.

Maasai Village Visit

Experience the rich traditions of the Maasai people, from their unique dances to traditional customs and clothing.

Swahili Culture

Discover the Swahili heritage in Mombasa, where you can explore ancient coastal towns, markets, and historical buildings.

Coastal Tribal Traditions

Engage with the Giriama and other coastal tribes to learn about their ancient rituals, music, and cuisine.
